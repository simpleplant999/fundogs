# What you can do on FunDogs

FunDogs helps people support animal rescue and rehoming through clear, trustworthy fundraising. Here is what the platform offers today—for visitors, donors, and people raising funds.

---

## For everyone (no account needed)

- **Home** — Learn what FunDogs is about, see how we verify people and campaigns, and browse featured fundraisers when they are live.
- **Donate** — Explore open fundraising campaigns and open the ones you care about.
- **Organizations** — Discover rescue groups and partners on the platform and see their public pages and campaigns.
- **How verification works** — Step-by-step explanation of how posts and campaigns are reviewed before they go public.
- **Terms & fees** — Plain-language summary of how fees work for campaigns and donations.
- **Contact us** — Send a message from the website—report a concern (such as suspected misuse) or ask a general question. You choose the topic and describe what happened; we do not open your email app for you.
- **Support FunDogs** — Optionally give to the platform itself (separate from animal campaigns), to help keep the site running.

---

## For donors and supporters

- **Campaign pages** — Read the story, see photos, track progress toward the goal, and view recent supporters (where shown).
- **Give safely** — Donate using supported payment options (such as QR/wallet or card, depending on setup). You can give in your own name or choose a more private option where available.
- **Share** — Use sharing tools on a campaign page to spread the word.
- **Updates** — Follow news and milestones creators add to their campaigns.

---

## For people with an account

- **Sign up & sign in** — Create a secure account with your email. If your group shared an **invite code**, you can use it when you register to join that organization automatically.
- **Profile** — Update your name and photo, and keep your password up to date.
- **After login** — You are taken to **My campaigns**, where you manage fundraisers you run.
- **Public profile** — Others can open a simple public page for you that lists campaigns you are associated with.

---

## For people raising funds

- **My campaigns** — See all your fundraisers in one place and start a **new campaign**.
- **Edit your campaign** — Update the story, photos, and goal; manage payout details and request withdrawals according to the platform rules.
- **Campaign updates** — Post short updates so donors see how things are going.

---

## For organization members

If you belong to a group on FunDogs (for example a rescue chapter):

- **Organization profile** — Help maintain how your group appears publicly—description, photos, and branding-style images where the platform allows.
- **Members** — See who is in the group; organization admins can manage member roles as the site allows.

---

## Trust and safety (what we say on the site)

- Campaigns are **reviewed** before they are shown publicly, using a process we describe on the **verification** page.
- We aim to **verify organizers** and organizations according to our rules before they can fundraise or represent a group.
- We take **reports seriously**—use **Contact us** if something looks wrong.

---

## Note

Some options depend on your region, payment setup, and whether your organizer account is approved. If something is not available on your screen, your local help or onboarding contact can point you to the next step.
